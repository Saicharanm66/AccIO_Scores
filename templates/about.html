ABOUT
The Göteborg Premier League (GPL), is a men's Twenty15 cricket Red Tennis ball league, contested by many teams based out of Gothenburg City, Sweden. The league was founded by the Göteborg Royals Cricket Association (Gothenburg Royals) in 2018. It is usually held between May, June and July of every year. The GPL is one of the most-attended cricket league in Gothenburg City. The brand value of the GPL in 2019 attracted so many sponsors i.e. Apna Bazaar and Spice on Wheels online grocery stores, Home Food agencies like Ruchi Foods, FoodExa, JSAN IT Consultancy and many more. The 2021 GPL season set a massive viewership record which attracted many new teams showed interest to participate There have been four seasons of the GPL tournament. The current IPL title holder franchise is Gully Cricketers, winning the 2021 season. GPL has an official committee who organizes the league, the name is League Committee (LG). GPL also does charity work and contributed to Covid Relief fund during last years pandemic.
